GLOBAL Trade Countries

VIETNAM

India and Vietnam have a growing trade relationship, with bilateral trade reaching $14.82 billion in 2023-2024—a 0.76% increase from the previous year.

India's exports to Vietnam amount to $5.47 billion, while imports from Vietnam stand at $9.34 billion.

Key Trade Statistics:

  • India's Ranking: 21st largest trading partner and 22nd largest export destination globally.
  • Vietnam's Ranking: 7th largest trading partner, 7th biggest importer, and 11th biggest import source.
  • Top Indian Exports: Frozen bovine meat, cereals, iron and steel, cotton, and animal fodder & materials.
  • Top Vietnamese Exports: Electronic equipment, telecom gear, machinery, and mechanical appliances.

Both India and Vietnam aim for a trade target of $15 billion, facilitated by entities such as the Joint Committee for Economic, Trade, Science, and Technology Cooperation (JCM) and the Sub-Committee on Trade.
